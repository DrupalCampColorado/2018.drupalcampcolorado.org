This Just In: DrupalCamp Colorado T-Shirt Design Revealed!

If the excitement of preparing for DrupalCamp Colorado 2018 in just under a month wasn’t already enough, this year’s t-shirt design has officially been revealed! When you register as a paid attendee you will receive one of these awesome camp t-shirts that you will be able to sport all weekend long (disclaimer:  we do encourage you to wash your t-shirt after every day).

If you haven’t already, head over to the registration page[link] and sign up now!! We have several tiers of registration:

  • Free Camp Registration - Free registration, no strings attached!
  • $25 Individual Sponsorship - Camp registration and DCCO 2018 custom t-shirt
  • $50 Individual Sponsorship - Camp registration, DCCO 2018 custom t-shirt, help subsidize free registrations!
  • $100 Individual Sponsorship - The ultimate individual sponsorship! Get a t-shirt from this year AND last year (sizes limited).

We are looking forward to seeing everyone at DrupalCamp Colorado 2018, August 3-5!
