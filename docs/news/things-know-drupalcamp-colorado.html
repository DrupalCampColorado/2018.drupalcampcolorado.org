Things To Know Before DrupalCamp Colorado

Things To Know Before DrupalCamp Colorado

The excitement is mounting as we get closer to DrupalCamp Colorado. We know that we are months away but there is quite a lot to get done before the first of our August sessions get underway. Development on this website is ongoing and we look forward to building out the registration sections, session submissions area, easy sponsorship opportunities and more! This is why you should check back often, bookmark this website in your browser, and begin planning for your visit to this year’s camp.

RSVP For DrupalCamp Colorado

As any great host knows, getting a sense of how many people will be at an event is important for plenty of reasons. With a number in mind of how many people may attend DrupalCamp Colorado, we can better serve you and make your stay here in Colorado, and at the camp - no matter how long or short - an optimal experience. Just as you may strive to plan and build your Drupal applications to the most optimal standards, we want your camp visit to be memorable for years to come. So please take the time to head over to our Meetup page and RSVP so we can get an idea of how many of you will be attending DrupalCamp Colorado, 2018.

Sponsorships

Not only do the event hosts like to gather information on how many people will attend an event, but events also benefit from sponsorships and businesses or individuals willing to become sponsors. This support also ensures that guests, speakers, and daily necessities are all available and taken care of. So if you, someone you know, or a business that you might have in mind are looking to get their name out there and are looking to support a great cause, have them fill out our sponsorship form today! The Drupal open-source community and the Drupal community right here in Colorado thrives off of volunteers and many great sponsors.

Join The Party

If you have never been to DrupalCamp Colorado, or any Drupal Camp before, you are in for quite a treat! Apart from the almost infinite knowledge gathered together in one place, there will be a lot of sessions for you to join in on, great opportunities to engage and network with people and businesses and of course, fun! Yes, the Drupal community, especially Colorado’s Drupal community, know how to throw a party. So if you are new to Drupal, new to Colorado, are an experienced Drupal developer, or are just curious and want to learn from professionals in their field, be sure to join us at Drupal Camp Colorado, August 3-5 at the King Center on Auraria Campus.

We cannot be more excited for this year’s Drupal Camp Colorado. Remember to save the dates in your calendar and to RSVP on our Meetup page. Invite your friends, your coworkers, your colleagues and businesses that you might know. Become a sponsor yourself or help us find a business that would like to sponsor the event. We look forward to seeing you there!
