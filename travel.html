Travel

DrupalCamp Colorado 2018 is held in downtown Denver on the Auraria Campus. Friday trainings and summits, Saturday sessions and keynotes, and Sunday's code sprints will take place in the King Center of the Auraria Higher Education Center, 855 Lawrence Way, Denver, CO.

The Saturday night party will be held at The Rio Grande Restaurant, a 15 minute walk from the venue.

Getting to the venue

Getting from Denver International Airport to downtown Denver is easy. Just take the A Train from DIA to Union Station and you're a few minutes walk from the venue. Union Station is convenient for many modes of mass transit with both bus and light rail stations departing frequently. You can use the above Google map to get directions from your home, then select "bus" mode to see options and schedule. RTD Denver has more details on public transport.

Parking

There are a number of lots available for parking on Auraria Campus. See their map of parking.

Biking

Ride your bike! The platte and Cherry Creek trails will both get you right near the campus from miles around. Also, there is a B-Cycle stations near King Center, Union Station, and throughout Denver if you'd like to rent a bike.
